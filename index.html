Data Science Projects

A selection of Data Science Projects built in Colab Goggle - Jupyter Notebook. Colab Goggle allows anybody to write and execute arbitrary python code through the browser, and is especially well suited to machine learning, data analysis and education.

Earthquakes with Magnitude 6 and higher since 1968

Earthquakes animation with geographical coordinates, magnitude and time series. Magnitude is normalize. Only earthquakes with magnitude bigger than 6 are plotted.

Another World Bank Project...Add A Little Bit Of Spice

Coursera Project in which a World Bank dataset is uploaded from the internet into Google Colab and cleaned. Statistical Analysis is performed including ANOVA.
