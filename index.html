Another World Bank Project...Add A Little Bit Of Spice

Coursera Project in which a World Bank dataset is uploaded from the internet into Google Colab and cleaned. Statistical Analysis is performed including ANOVA. For clarity purposes, ANOVA step-by-step calculation is included. To enhance the visuals, folium clorometh map is presented with a selection of tiles.

Folium Plugins: HeatMapWithTime, Earthquakes Magnitude across the world since 1970

Earthquakes are displayed on a HeatMap With Time. The Display Index (Date and Time) is displayed on a widget that can be moved backward, forward, stop, move till the end of beginning of the simulation. The Display Index is located in the down left side of the map. The fps can be customized as well to speed up the simulation. The map has a zoom in/out feature. Each earthquake has a color-gradient to display its Magnitude. The gradient colors are: 1: Navy, 2: Blue, 4: Lime, 5: Green, 6: Orange, 7: Red.
